ROOMS & SPACES

The Realm of the Mind Libraries have several rooms available for group study or meeting rooms and scheduled lectures. Read on for details on how to book a room.

STUDY ROOMS
Availability of group classroom libraries at multiple library locations. There are 16 divided study rooms and large group lecture rooms located north of the library on the first floor, north and center on the second floor, as well as a large theater halls below the building. These group study rooms provide enclosed spaces for groups of at least 6 people to work together, lecture rooms have a minimum of 20 people, and the theater halls accommodates 200 people. Students or library clients can use the group study rooms for academic or practical use for a maximum of two hours per day and book from the library only for two hours or for the whole day via the site. Priority is given to students. As for lecture rooms and theater, they are booked for one or more days through the website.
Using ROOMS/HALLS POLICIES & PROCEDURES
  • Group study rooms/halls can be booked online or in the desired study room by scanning a QR code using a mobile device. While on the Internet, you must log in using the email address of the site The Realm of the Mind. Users can make one (1) reservation for day at minimum to a ten days at maximum in the same reservation.
  • A minimum of two (2) people is required to be able to reserve and use the study room. Add their email to the list of participants on the right of the booking screen.
  • Room reservations not claimed after 15 minutes from the start are subject to cancellation.
  • Rooms are available on a first-come-first-served basis when not booked.
  • For assistance in using the online reservation system, please speak to a staff member at the service desks.
  • While in classrooms, please keep conversations on a moderate level so that other beneficiaries are not disturbed.

Capacity: Minimum of 2 individuals up to 6 in small rooms, between 10 and 50 persons for large rooms, and up to 200 person in the big hall.

  • Must be SBU affiliated
  • 1 Flat screen monitor and LCD at most of rooms.
  • Whiteboard in all rooms.
  • Built in cable adapters, and power outlets.
  • Comfortable chairs.
  • Music for reading.

  • Food & Noise Policy: Zone rules apply; please discard of all food wrappers and trash in the garbage bins.
  • Supplies: Whiteboard markers, erasers, and adapter cables are available for checkout
  • Note: Failure to adhere to study room and library policies may result in loss of study room privileges.
